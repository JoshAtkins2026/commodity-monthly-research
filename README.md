# commodity-monthly-research
Commodity Markets Monthly Research | Python
A monthly research log tracking commodity market performance, cross-asset correlations and spread dynamics across Energy, Metals, Softs, Grains and Macro.
Structure:

Automated monthly report generated by Python covering Brent-WTI spread, volatility regime, Z score signal and daily performance across 15 commodities
Written analyst commentary published each month covering market overview, key risks and outlook

Coverage: Brent, WTI, Natural Gas, Gold, Silver, Copper, Platinum, Sugar, Coffee, Cotton, Wheat, Corn, Soybeans, DXY, S&P 500
Tools: Python, pandas, NumPy, matplotlib
Updated: Monthly
